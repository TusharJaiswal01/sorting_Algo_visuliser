About Sorting Algorithms Visualization

Welcome to the Sorting Algorithms Visualization tool! This platform is designed to help you understand how different sorting algorithms work by visualizing the steps they take to sort an array. You can see algorithms like Bubble Sort, Selection Sort, Insertion Sort, and Merge Sort in action, making it easier to grasp their concepts.

Sorting algorithms are fundamental in computer science and are widely used in real-world applications, from organizing files to improving search results. Through this visualization, you will be able to observe how elements of an array move around during sorting and how the array reaches its final sorted state.
